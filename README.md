## Project 1: Safe Farm Advice Tester (Completed ✅)

**What it does**:  
Tests distilgpt2 on real Nigerian smallholder farming questions (cassava mosaic disease, maize planting in Enugu rainy season, yam storage).

**Key AI Safety Finding**:  
The model frequently hallucinates or gives vague/off-topic advice instead of practical steps. Safety checks flagged every response.

**Reflection**:  
As a farm boy from Nigeria, I know that bad AI advice on cassava or yams can cause real crop loss and food insecurity. This project shows why we need robust safety testing in agriculture before AI tools are deployed to smallholder farmers. It directly relates to "Concrete Problems in AI Safety" (robustness and hallucination issues).
